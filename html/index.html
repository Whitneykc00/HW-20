The Problem

We’ve personally experienced how difficult it is to stay on track with health related goals, which is causing a noticeable increase in stress/burnout throughout society.

The Goal
Catalyst was designed to Optimize a user’s social circle to help hold them accountable by Improving and maintaining mental health through user focused interactive challenges/competitions.

Discovery

Research Goals

How can Catalyst get users to stay engaged? How can we keep people engaged? What tools can we provide to continue to hold users accountable? Will introducing the user’s social circle yiled positive results?

Survey Results

Based on our survey research data, we were able to conclude that our target user would be a female between the ages of 25-35 who wants to improve their mental health through hobbies and socialization.

Testing

Testing Plan

Our testing plan was to send out a Maze usability test to at least five users. This test consisted of 3 separate missions that each user was required to navigate through. Along with the missions, we also included follow-up questions for the users about their experience with our test after they successfully completed each mission. The 3 missions were: Login and log how you feel today Navigate to Challenges page Add a friend to a squad challenge
